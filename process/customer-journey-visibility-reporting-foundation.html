Tool Selection: Which Platform?

Outreach: Enterprise-grade, powerful automation, best for complex sales orgs. $100-150/user/mo.

SalesLoft: Strong on cadence + dialer integration, great UX. Similar pricing to Outreach.

Apollo: All-in-one prospecting + engagement, includes data. Best value for early-stage. $49-79/user/mo.

HubSpot Sequences: If you're already on HubSpot, solid for simple use cases. Included in Sales Pro+.

Our take: For Series A/B with SDR teams under 10 people, Apollo or HubSpot Sequences are usually enough. For 15+ SDRs or enterprise sales with complex multi-threading, Outreach or SalesLoft justify the cost.

Real Client Example

Series B Dev Tools, $9M ARR:

Had 6 SDRs using Outreach, but it was basically an expensive email sender. No structured sequences. Everyone built their own cadences. No consistency. Connect rates were 2-3%.

After Engagement Platform Optimization:
