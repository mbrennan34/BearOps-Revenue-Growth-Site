Sales Playbooks & Lead-To-Cash Workflows

Codifying the “one way we sell” and wiring it into your tools so every rep follows the same path from lead to signed contract and handoff.

The Problem: Your best reps close in spite of the system, not because of it. Everyone has their own version of discovery, qualification, and proposal. Handoffs to CS/Finance are messy, and data in the CRM doesn’t match what’s actually happening in deals.

Why This Matters

Without a standardised sales process and lead-to-cash workflow, every new hire is a new experiment. Coaching is vague, forecasting is unreliable, and cross-functional coordination relies on individual heroics.

How This Works in Practice

Week 1: Map the Real Process

  • Interview top performers and managers to capture how deals really get done today
  • Review current stages, fields and handoffs in CRM / billing tools
  • Identify friction points and failure modes across teams

Week 2: Design the Target Flow

  • Define target sales stages and exit criteria
  • Create playbooks and mutual action plan templates
  • Design lead-to-cash workflow with clear roles and SLAs

Week 3: Tooling & Training

  • Configure CRM fields, stages and automation
  • Run live enablement sessions with reps and managers
  • Agree KPIs and review rhythm for continuous improvement
