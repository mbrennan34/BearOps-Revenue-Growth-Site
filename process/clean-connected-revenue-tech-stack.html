HubSpot vs Salesforce: Which One?

HubSpot: Great for startups, SMB sales, marketing-heavy orgs. Easier to use, lower cost, faster to implement. Limitations kick in at complex deal flows, enterprise sales, heavy customization needs.

Salesforce: Better for complex enterprise sales, multi-touch attribution, heavy custom workflows. Higher cost, steeper learning curve, but scales to infinite complexity.

Our recommendation: For Series A/B companies under $15M ARR with velocity sales motion (sub-90 day cycles), HubSpot is usually the right call. For enterprise sales with 6+ month cycles and complex buying centers, Salesforce wins.
