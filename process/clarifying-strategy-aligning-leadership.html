Component 1: Strategic Clarity & Leadership Alignment

Clarifying Strategy & Aligning the Leadership Team

Define WHO you are, WHERE you're going, and HOW you'll win. Get leadership aligned on purpose, vision, and strategy before you scale.

The Problem We Solve:

Most leadership teams have vague answers to fundamental questions: "Who are we for?" becomes "mid-market SaaS." "Why do we win?" becomes "great product." "Where are we going?" becomes "grow revenue." That's not strategy...it's hope. Without brutal clarity, every deal feels custom, every hire is a debate, and scaling means chaos.

The 3 Strategic Pillars

Every scaling company needs to answer three fundamental questions. We've organized the entire VTO framework around these pillars.

WHO ARE WE?

Define your company's identity and values before you scale. If your team can't articulate why you exist beyond making money, you'll struggle to attract talent and customers who believe what you believe.

What We Build:
  • Core Purpose ‚Äì Your "WHY" using Simon Sinek's Golden Circle
  • Core Values ‚Äì 3-5 non-negotiable behaviors that define your culture
AI Generators:
  • AI Purpose Generator (3 statement options)
  • AI Values Generator (suggested behaviors)

WHERE ARE WE GOING?

Create a vivid, measurable vision that cascades from 10 years to this quarter. Without this clarity, every team sets different goals and you lose alignment at scale.

What We Build:
  • 10-Year BHAG ‚Äì Your mountain top target (Jim Collins framework)
  • 3-Year Picture ‚Äì Vivid vision of future state (Cameron Herold)
  • 1-Year Goals ‚Äì 3-7 SMART measurable objectives
AI Generators:
  • AI BHAG Generator (4 BHAG types)
  • AI Picture Generator (vivid vision builder)
  • AI Goals Generator (SMART goal suggestions)

HOW DO WE WIN?

Define your ICP, strategic bets, and competitive positioning. This is where most companies stay vague. "We sell to mid-market" isn't an ICP. "We're better" isn't positioning.

What We Build:
  • ICP / Target Market ‚Äì Who succeeds wildly with you
  • Strategic Bets ‚Äì 3-5 major resource investments
  • Differentiation ‚Äì Why choose you (April Dunford framework)
AI Generators:
  • AI ICP Recommender (market analysis)
  • AI Strategic Bets Generator (investment priorities)
  • AI Positioning Advisor (competitive differentiation)

Why This Structure Works

The 3 pillars create a logical flow for your leadership workshop:

  1. WHO establishes identity and culture (foundation)
  2. WHERE sets the vision and goals (direction)
  3. HOW defines the strategy to get there (execution)

Each pillar builds on the previous one. You can't define strategic bets (HOW) until you know your 1-year goals (WHERE). You can't set meaningful goals until you know your purpose (WHO).

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Strategic Clarity & Leadership Alignment workshop.

How is this different from a normal strategy consulting engagement? +

Traditional strategy consulting gives you a 60-slide deck after 8 weeks of interviews. We give you working tools, AI-powered generators, and completed frameworks in 1-2 days. You leave with artifacts you can actually use and update‚Äînot a PDF that sits in Google Drive.

Plus, our focus is on leadership alignment, not just strategy definition. The best strategy in the world doesn't matter if your exec team isn't bought in.

Do we need to have our strategy figured out before the workshop? +

No‚Äîthat's the point. Most companies come to us with vague strategy ("sell to mid-market SaaS") and misaligned leadership (CEO thinks one thing, CRO thinks another). We use the workshop to create that clarity and alignment.

The AI generators help accelerate decisions even if you're starting from scratch. That said, some pre-work helps: completing the Pre-Assessment and having 1-2 examples of customers who've succeeded wildly with you.

How many people should attend the workshop? +

Ideal: 3-7 people (founders + executive team). This usually includes: CEO, CRO/VP Sales, CMO/VP Marketing, CPO/VP Product, and sometimes CFO or Head of CS.

Too small (just founders) and you miss critical perspectives. Too large (12+ people) and you can't make decisions. If you have a bigger team, do the workshop with executives and then do a separate alignment session with the broader leadership team.

What if we already have a mission statement / values / strategy? +

Perfect‚Äîwe'll start there. The Pre-Assessment will show us where you have strong alignment (green) vs. weak alignment (yellow/red). We focus the workshop on the yellow/red areas.

For example, if your purpose and values are solid but you're fuzzy on ICP and positioning, we'll spend 80% of time on Pillar 3 (HOW DO WE WIN) and just validate Pillar 1 (WHO ARE WE).

Can we do this async / remote or does it need to be in-person? +

The workshop itself should be synchronous (live video or in-person). These are hard conversations‚Äîyou need the debate, the whiteboarding, the "wait, what do you mean by that?" moments.

That said, all the pre-work and follow-up can be async. The interactive tools work great remotely. And if you absolutely can't get everyone together, we can break it into 3-4 half-day sessions over a week.
