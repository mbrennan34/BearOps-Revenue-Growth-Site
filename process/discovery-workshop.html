Growth Blocker Analysis
Identify and prioritise the 1–2 critical obstacles preventing you from hitting your growth goals in the next 12–18 months.
▼

In the room, we work with your leadership team to:

  • Surface the biggest sources of friction across marketing, sales, and CS.
  • Map each blocker to a specific part of the revenue engine.
  • Score impact vs. effort so we focus on the 1–2 issues that really matter.

After the session, you leave with:

  • A prioritised list of growth blockers with clear owners.
  • A 90-day focus plan that links blockers to actions and metrics.
  • A proposal from BearOps showing how we can help remove those blockers.
