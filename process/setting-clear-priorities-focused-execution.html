How The Workshop Works

Click to see the 4-session breakdown (4-5 hours total)

+

Pre-Workshop (30 minutes async)

Leadership team completes execution assessment individually. Identifies current # of priorities, execution pain points, and readiness gaps. AI generates custom insights for workshop focus.

Session 1: Priority Capture (90 minutes)

Live Lucid board session where every leader adds priorities under Growth/Product/Ops/People. No judgment, just capture. Themes emerge naturally. We document conflicts and surprises.

Session 2: Driver Synthesis (60 minutes)

Group wall items into Revenue, Product, Operations, People drivers. AI identifies 3-5 execution themes. Map constraints that will block progress (people, process, tools, focus).

Session 3: Voting & Rocks (90 minutes)

Convert drivers into 8-15 discrete priority options. Leadership dot voting (5-7 votes each). Top 5 emerge. Auto-generate 3-5 Quarterly Rocks with owners and timelines.

Session 4: Execution Design (60 minutes)

For each Rock, define lead indicators (weekly tracking) and lag indicators (quarterly outcomes). Build execution scorecard. Create communication plan and 30-day rollout.

Total Time Investment: 4-5 hours over 2 weeks (typically 2-3 sessions) + 30 minutes pre-work per leader

Frequently Asked Questions

OKR vs EOS, remote workshops, dealing with disagreement, and more

+

How is this different from regular OKR or EOS implementation?

We don't force a framework on you. We use EOS-inspired Rocks but customize the process to your stage, team dynamics, and constraints. The interactive tool guides the workshop in real-time rather than using generic templates. And we facilitate the first full cycle so you see it work in practice.

What if our leadership team disagrees on priorities?

That's exactly why you need this workshop! The dot voting process surfaces disagreement transparently. When someone's priority gets 2% of votes, that's data‚Äînot politics. The constraint mapping helps explain WHY certain priorities aren't viable. Disagreement becomes productive discussion.

Can we do this remotely?

Yes. The Priority Wall uses Lucid board (collaborative online canvas). Voting happens in the interactive tool. Sessions work great on Zoom with screen sharing. Many clients prefer remote because it's easier to schedule and people are less shy about voting honestly.

What if we already have quarterly planning in place?

Great! We'll audit your current process and enhance it rather than replace it. Often the issue isn't lack of planning‚Äîit's too many priorities, unclear owners, or weak follow-through. We strengthen the weakest links.

How long until we see results?

Immediate clarity after the workshop (day 1). First Rock progress visible within 2 weeks. Meaningful impact by 30 days when the execution rhythm is embedded. Full quarterly cycle (90 days) to prove the system works.
