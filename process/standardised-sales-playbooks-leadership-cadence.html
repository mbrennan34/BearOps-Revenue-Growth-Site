One way to sell. Consistent discovery. Repeatable deal progression. No more "winging it."

The Problem: Every rep sells differently. Your best rep closes at 40%, your worst at 12%. You can't coach or scale what isn't documented. Marketing hands off leads, Sales says they're garbage, CS inherits broken customer expectations. Nothing connects.

Why This Matters

Founder-led sales works because founders are brilliant improvisers. They read the room, pivot the pitch, close on instinct. But you can't hire 10 more founders. You need a repeatable methodology that average performers can execute at high success rates.

Without documented playbooks, every rep reinvents the wheel. Discovery calls are random. Demo flow depends on mood. Deal progression is guesswork. Managers can't coach because there's no standard to coach to.
