Philosophy

Why this approach works

Every outbound motion starts with the same question: "Who is the persona?" and "What trigger makes them care right now?"

  • AI generates baseline research, discovery, and messaging in 8 minutes
  • Manual modules (02-03) let you refine and customize as needed
  • Discovery frameworks are written as if you already have the meeting
  • Templates are trigger narratives, not generic best practices
  • Calls, LinkedIn, and emails all reuse the same core messaging
